PRONUNCIATION Can/Can´t

*Can is usually unestressed in positive sentences
 Can = (Ken)  EG:You can take photos.
 If you stress "Can" the listener may think you are
  saying a negative sentence.

*Can´t is always stressed in negative  senteces
 Not stressing the listener may understand a positive sentence
 Can´t= (Ként) in USA
 Can´t= (Kant) in Uk

 Note; 
* Can is stressed in yes/no questions
  Eg: Can I sit here?/ Can I park here?
      (Kánai) sit here?
   
    But not in questions begining with a question word
  Eg:Where can I sit?/ Where can I park?
           (kanai)
